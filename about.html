About Floofyware Research

There hasn't been much research or advancement in training large deep learning models on very limited computing power, as it has often been overlooked recently in favor of scaling up models and training them with terabytes of data. I want to change that by training powerful models purely on my personal workstation, using optimizations everywhere to get the most out of the hardware.

I will release every model I create as open-source, including training data, except for some experimental models that might use data from artists I work with to respect their wishes and work. However, all the research and details will be published for others to build upon for those models still.

I firmly believe that fully open-source AI is the path forward for us all to develop universally safer and more ethical AI to improve the quality of humanity while mitigating the existential risks that come with this technology. I also want to show that powerful models can be created with very limited computing power, as not everyone has the money to purchase or rent powerful hardware to perform experiments and develop their own models.

My goal is to help develop safer, better, and environmentally friendly AI by focusing on data quality and algorithmic improvements rather than heavy scaling. I will leave no GPU idle!
